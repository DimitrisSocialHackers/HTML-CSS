The resources and software programs that Social Hackers Academy instructors urged me to use are the following:

  • Slack:A free messaging app for teams. Useful to send questions to instructors and get replies real-time.
  • GitHub:A free development platform which helps you to build better software.
  • Trello:A free web-based Project Management application. It helps see where the team is and give and get feedback to and from colleagues.
  • Visual Studio IDE: A free Microsoft product that enables you to write code accurately and effectively.
  • Khan Academy:A site offering free online courses on HMTL and CSS. It is a prerequisite to complete a few courses in order to join the Full Stack Web Development Course by Social Hackers Academy non-profit entity.
